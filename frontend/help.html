Frequently asked questions

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Some categories (intimates, cosmetics once opened) may not be returnable. Check product page for details.
Once we receive the returned item, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
Can I change my shipping address after order?
If the order is not yet shipped, contact support quickly using the chat or phone. Once shipped, address cannot be changed.
